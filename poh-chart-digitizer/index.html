POH Chart Digitizer

1. Load Chart Image

2. Calibrate Right Side

Click X points (weight axis), then Y points (distance axis). Y points define the shared pixel heights for both sides.

3. Calibrate Left Side

Click X points (temperature axis) on the chart. No Y calibration needed — the crossover uses pixel heights directly from the right side.

4. Trace Altitude Lines — Left Side

Select an altitude, click along its line from left to right.

5. Trace Reference Curves — Right Side

Trace the guide curves. These define the interpolation field — they are not paired with altitudes.

6. Compute & Export

For each temp+altitude, crosses to the right side and reads distance at each weight.
